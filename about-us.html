Our Story
We are focused on improving credit availability for users from tier-2 & tier-3 cities. Currently, lower & mid-income segment groups don't have access to high-quality financial services, low ticket sizes and being non-focus groups for banks. We believe by leveraging technology we could build a self-serve system that could deliver a great experience while ensuring we capture sufficient data points for underwriting credits.
Our first credit product is a Gold Loan.
Currently, 65% of the $130 billion gold loan market is unorganised (bypassing regulations) & managed by pawnbrokers, money lenders, and jewellers, amongst others. In the unorganised market, customers are exploited frequently with high-interest rates (>30%).
We are building technology which enables jewelers & pawnbrokers to work with multiple banks & convert unorganised businesses into organised ones with no hassle. This will also reduce the interest rates charged to end customers.
